📚 Wiki-Read

A beautiful Wikipedia client with games, accounts, gangs, and economy! 🚀

✨ Features

📖 Wikipedia Reader

· Full articles with proper formatting
· 30+ languages supported (🇷🇺 🇬🇧 🇩🇪 🇫🇷 and more)
· Images, tables, and working links
· Search and random article buttons
· Bookmark your favorite articles

👥 Accounts & Gangs

· Create your own profile
· Choose your avatar and join a GANG: 😎 Cool Kids, 🤖 Robots, 👻 Ghosts, 🦊 Foxes, 🐼 Pandas and many more!
· Gang leaderboards – compete for the top spot!
· Follow other users

📝 User Articles

· Write and publish your own articles
· Like and share posts
· Edit or delete your content
· Community feed with latest articles

💰 Economy System

· Level up by reading and interacting
· Earn 🪙 Coins for every action
· Collect rare 💸 Shik currency
· Daily quests with big rewards
· Exchange 100 🪙 → 1 💸
· Shop with exclusive items

🎮 Mini-Games

· 🖼️ Guess the Article – Identify articles from images
· 🧩 Fact Puzzle – Match facts to correct articles
· 🤔 Truth or Myth – Separate facts from fiction
· ⚔️ Title Battle – Choose the right title for descriptions

🧮 Tools

· Scientific calculator with history
· Stopwatch & timer
· Export articles to TXT
· Speech synthesis (30+ languages)
· Dark/Light theme
· Reading progress indicator

🚀 Quick Start

1. Download the latest release
2. Extract the archive
3. Open index.html in your browser

That's it! No server or build tools required.

📱 Mobile Friendly

Works perfectly on all devices: phones, tablets, and desktops.

🛠️ Built With

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Wikipedia API, LocalStorage
